<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Cyber-Brutalism meets Data Viz
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Raw Transparency**: Expose the "guts" of the AI evaluation process. No hiding behind smooth loaders; show the data streams.
  2. **High Contrast & Monospace**: Use stark contrast to highlight security vulnerabilities. Monospace fonts for code and data to emphasize precision.
  3. **Grid-Based but Broken**: A rigid grid structure that is intentionally disrupted by "glitch" effects or overlapping elements to represent the chaotic nature of security threats.
  4. **Information Density**: Maximize data visibility without clutter. Use sparklines, heatmaps, and dense data tables.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Background**: Deep Void Black (#050505) - representing the unknown depth of LLM models.
  - **Primary**: Terminal Green (#00FF41) - classic hacker aesthetic, representing safe/verified status.
  - **Alert**: Glitch Red (#FF003C) - for vulnerabilities and critical failures.
  - **Accent**: Electric Blue (#00F0FF) - for active agents and data flow.
  - *Reasoning*: Evokes the feeling of a high-stakes security command center.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Dashboard-First**: No traditional landing page. The user is immediately dropped into a command center.
  - **Modular Panes**: Draggable, resizable panes for different agents (Pen-Tester, Fact-Checker, etc.).
  - **Sidebar-less**: Navigation is top-bar or context-menu driven to maximize screen real estate for data.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1. **"The Pulse"**: A rhythmic, subtle background animation that speeds up when agents are active.
  2. **ASCII Art Headers**: Section titles rendered in stylized ASCII or pixelated fonts.
  3. **Scanlines**: Subtle CRT scanline overlay on specific data panels.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Tactile Feedback**: Hover states trigger "decoding" text effects.
  - **Instant Response**: Buttons feel mechanical (click sounds, instant color inversion).

  **Animation**:
  - **Glitch Transitions**: Page loads and state changes involve brief digital distortion.
  - **Typewriter Effect**: Text streams in like a terminal output.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: `Space Mono` (Bold, Uppercase) - Technical and authoritative.
  - **Body**: `JetBrains Mono` - Optimized for code and readability.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Glassmorphism / Ethereal Security
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Transparency & Trust**: Use frosted glass effects to symbolize seeing *through* the AI models.
  2. **Soft but Strong**: Soft gradients and blurs combined with sharp, thin borders to denote precision.
  3. **Layered Depth**: Use Z-axis heavily. Agents sit on different "planes" of existence.
  4. **Fluid Intelligence**: Organic shapes and motions to represent the fluid nature of AI.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Background**: Deep Navy/Indigo Gradient (#0f172a to #1e1b4b) - Professional, secure, deep.
  - **Glass**: White with low opacity and high blur.
  - **Gradients**: Aurora Borealis colors (Teal, Purple, Pink) for AI visualization.
  - *Reasoning*: Security doesn't have to look scary. It can look sophisticated and enlightened.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Floating Cards**: Content lives in floating glass cards over a dynamic background.
  - **Central Focus**: The "Target LLM" is always center stage, with auditor agents orbiting it.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1. **"The Orb"**: A central, glowing orb representing the LLM being audited, changing color based on health score.
  2. **Frosted Borders**: 1px white borders with varying opacity.
  3. **Glowing Traces**: Connecting lines between agents that glow when data is transferred.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Smooth & Float**: Hovering lifts elements gently.
  - **Ripple Effects**: Clicks generate subtle ripples.

  **Animation**:
  - **Slow Breathing**: Background gradients slowly shift and breathe.
  - **Float**: Elements have a subtle, continuous floating animation.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: `Inter` (Tight tracking, Light weight) - Modern, clean.
  - **Body**: `Satoshi` or `Plus Jakarta Sans` - Geometric and friendly.
</idea>

<idea>
  **Design Movement**: Swiss Style / International Typographic Style (Neo-Brutalist twist)
  **Core Principles**:
  1. **Grid is God**: Absolute adherence to a strict grid system.
  2. **Typography as Image**: Large, bold typography is the main visual element.
  3. **Asymmetry**: Dynamic, asymmetrical layouts to create tension and interest.
  4. **Clarity & Objectivity**: No decorative elements. Form follows function.

  **Color Philosophy**:
  - **Background**: Stark White (#FFFFFF) or Off-White (#F0F0F0).
  - **Text**: Pure Black (#000000).
  - **Accents**: Primary Blue (#0055FF) and Warning Orange (#FF5500).
  - *Reasoning*: Clinical, objective, and highly legible. Perfect for an "Audit" tool.

  **Layout Paradigm**:
  - **Split Screen**: Vertical dividers splitting the screen into clear functional zones.
  - **Sticky Headers**: Large, sticky headers that follow the user.

  **Signature Elements**:
  1. **Thick Dividers**: Heavy black lines separating sections.
  2. **Oversized Numbers**: Audit scores displayed in massive font sizes.
  3. **Marquee**: Scrolling text for status updates (stock ticker style).

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - **Snap**: Scroll snapping to grid sections.
  - **Hover Reveal**: Hovering over a score reveals the detailed breakdown in a popover.

  **Animation**:
  - **Slide & Reveal**: Panels slide in from sides.
  - **Hard Cuts**: No fades. Instant changes.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: `Helvetica Now` or `Neue Haas Grotesk` (Heavy) - The classic Swiss choice.
  - **Body**: `Inter` or `Roboto` - Neutral and readable.
</idea>
